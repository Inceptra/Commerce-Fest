    About

    Scandalous tabloids, scathing exposés and ludicrously fake media are just the beginning in this fast paced event based on testing the contestant’s skills in dealing with the new age consumer base.

    You could be the one to dramatically alter the face of media, journalism and advertising with Avant Garde. Our creative event encourages participants to create works of previously unprecedented calibre by exploring the boundaries of creative freedom with our unconventional rounds and challenging succession of events.

    Rules


    Participants are required to carry props (if any) for round 1.
    A camera is essential for this event.

    Note: Contesting teams must contact the school two days prior to the event for allotment of topics for round 1.
