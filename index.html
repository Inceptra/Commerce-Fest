This Is Inceptra.

Inceptra is National Public School HSR layout’s first ever commerce fest to be held on 28 th July 2017. Working to build solidarity between students of different schools and disciplines, this fest is packed with a variety of events, each with their own challenges to stimulate contestants in every way possible. Over the course of the day, students from a multiplicity of schools will congregate on our campus to participate in this celebration of the world of commerce.
